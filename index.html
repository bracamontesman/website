Hello there!

I'm Miguel Bracamontes (@bracamontesman everywhere). Welcome to my online space! This is mostly a consolidation of cool old-fashioned links in an attempt to officialize my online presence. My blog is where I share information about myself, my thoughts, my journey in life, and some of my interests.

I'm a 30-year-old self-taught web enthusiast running this website, which is basically all about me and the things I like. I have a bachelor's degree in linguistics, but I have a computer for a heart and a huge knack for understanding tech and troubleshooting issues. I used to have an account on every major social network, and bits of it are forever cached on Google and the Internet Archive, so my old photo is kind of like an Easter egg.

There have been several other iterations of this website, but they were written in other languages and are no longer active. Their memories will only live on in the Wayback Machine.

I prioritize authenticity and simplicity, avoiding any use of content generation and AI.
